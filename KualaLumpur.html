Petronas Twin Towers

About Petronas Twin Towers

The Petronas Twin Towers, standing at 452 meters, were the tallest buildings in the world from 1998 to 2004. These iconic towers remain the tallest twin towers globally and a symbol of Malaysia's modernization. The towers feature Islamic-inspired geometric designs reflecting Malaysia's Muslim heritage.

Visitors can enjoy the sky bridge connecting the towers on the 41st and 42nd floors, offering breathtaking views of Kuala Lumpur. The towers are also home to Suria KLCC, a premier shopping mall, and the KLCC Park at their base.

Tips for Visitors

Visit in the evening to see the towers illuminated, and don't miss the nightly fountain show at KLCC Park. Dress modestly as it's an official building, and arrive early for the best photo opportunities.
